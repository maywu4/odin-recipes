Fruit Salad

Learn how to make a sweet fruit salad, perfect for as a healthy dessert!

Ingredients:

  • 1 mango
  • 6 strawberries
  • handful of blueberries
  • 3 pineapple slices
  • 4 tablespoons of maple syrup

Steps to Follow:

  1. Cut the mango,strawberries and pineapple into bite sized pieces.
  2. Add all the fruit into a bowl.
  3. Drizzle the maple syrup on top and mix.
