Peanut Butter and Jelly Sandwich

Learn how to make a grilled peanut butter and jelly sandwich!

Ingredients:

  • 2 slices of bread
  • 4 slices of butter
  • peanut butter
  • jelly

Steps to Follow:

  1. Smear a slice of butter on each side of the bread slices.
  2. Put the bread on the grill, flipping sides after 30 seconds on each side.
  3. Remove the bread from the grill and put peanut butter on one slice and jelly on the other slice.
  4. Combine both slices into a sandwich, cut diagonally and enjoy.
